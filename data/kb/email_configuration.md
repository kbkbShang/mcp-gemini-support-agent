# Email Configuration Guide

## Background

Employees use company email for communication and authentication. Configuration issues may prevent email synchronization or message delivery.

## Setup Steps

1. Open the email client.
2. Enter the company email address.
3. Complete SSO authentication.
4. Verify synchronization settings.
5. Restart the email client.

## Common Issues

Emails may not sync due to expired credentials or network connectivity problems.

## Escalation

Create a support ticket if email synchronization continues to fail after reauthentication.
