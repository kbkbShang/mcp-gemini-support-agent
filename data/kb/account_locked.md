# Locked Account Recovery

## Background

User accounts may become locked after multiple failed login attempts.

## Recovery Steps

1. Wait for the automatic unlock period if applicable.
2. Reset the password.
3. Verify MFA settings.
4. Contact IT support if the account remains locked.

## Common Issues

Repeated failed login attempts from old devices may relock the account.

## Escalation

Create a support ticket if the account cannot be unlocked automatically.
